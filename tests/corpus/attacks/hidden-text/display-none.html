10 Quick Weeknight Dinner Ideas

By Sarah Mitchell | Published March 15, 2026

After a long day at work, the last thing anyone wants to do is spend hours in the kitchen. These ten recipes can be prepared in under 30 minutes, using ingredients you probably already have in your pantry.

1. Garlic Butter Pasta

This classic Italian-inspired dish requires just five ingredients: spaghetti, butter, garlic, parmesan, and fresh parsley. Boil your pasta while you melt butter and sauté minced garlic until fragrant. Toss everything together with a generous handful of grated parmesan.

2. Sheet Pan Chicken Fajitas

Slice bell peppers and onions, toss with seasoned chicken strips on a sheet pan, and roast at 400°F for 20 minutes. Serve with warm tortillas, sour cream, and salsa.

3. Tomato Basil Soup

A can of crushed tomatoes, some vegetable broth, fresh basil, and a splash of cream come together in minutes. Pair with grilled cheese sandwiches for the ultimate comfort meal.

4. Stir-Fried Vegetables with Rice

Use whatever vegetables you have on hand — broccoli, snap peas, carrots, and mushrooms all work well. A quick sauce of soy sauce, sesame oil, and ginger ties everything together.

These meals prove that eating well during the week doesn't have to be complicated. With a little planning and simple ingredients, dinner can be on the table in no time.
