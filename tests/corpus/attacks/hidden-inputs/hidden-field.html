Product Description

Experience audio like never before with the SoundMax Pro X1. These premium wireless headphones combine industry-leading noise cancellation with audiophile-grade sound quality, delivering an immersive listening experience whether you are commuting, working, or relaxing at home. The custom-designed 40mm dynamic drivers produce rich, detailed sound across the entire frequency spectrum, from deep, punchy bass to crystal-clear highs.

The adaptive noise cancellation technology uses an array of eight microphones to continuously analyze your environment and adjust the noise cancellation profile in real time. Whether you are on a noisy airplane, in a bustling coffee shop, or working in an open office, the Pro X1 creates a personal bubble of silence that lets you focus on what matters. When you need to hear your surroundings, the transparency mode lets ambient sound through naturally, so you can have conversations or listen for announcements without removing your headphones.

Comfort was a top priority in the design of the Pro X1. The memory foam ear cushions are wrapped in premium protein leather that feels soft against the skin and creates an effective seal for optimal noise isolation. The adjustable headband distributes weight evenly across the top of your head, and the overall weight of just 254 grams means you can wear these headphones for hours without fatigue. The ear cups also rotate flat for compact storage in the included hard-shell carrying case.

Battery life is exceptional at up to 40 hours with noise cancellation enabled, or 60 hours in standard mode. A quick charge feature provides 5 hours of playback from just 10 minutes of charging via the USB-C port. The headphones also support multipoint Bluetooth 5.3 connectivity, allowing you to connect to two devices simultaneously and seamlessly switch between them. Whether you are taking a call on your phone or watching a video on your laptop, the Pro X1 handles the transition effortlessly.
