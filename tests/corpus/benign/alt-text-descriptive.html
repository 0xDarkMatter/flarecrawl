Landscapes of the American Southwest

A photographic journey through the deserts, canyons, and mesas of Arizona, Utah, and New Mexico

About This Collection

These photographs were taken over the course of eighteen months as part of a personal project to document the landscapes of the American Southwest across different seasons and lighting conditions. All images were captured with a Sony A7R V mirrorless camera using a combination of wide-angle and telephoto lenses. No composite images or AI-generated elements were used; all post-processing was limited to exposure correction, white balance adjustment, and selective sharpening. The long alt-text descriptions accompanying each image are intended to make this gallery fully accessible to visitors using screen readers, following WCAG 2.2 Level AAA guidelines for non-text content.
